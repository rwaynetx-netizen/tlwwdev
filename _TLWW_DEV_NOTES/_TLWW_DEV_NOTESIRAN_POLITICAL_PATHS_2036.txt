TLWW DEVELOPMENT NOTE
Iran Political Paths – 2036 Scenario Design

Date Logged: 2026
Author: TLWW Development

Purpose
Define plausible political trajectories for Iran in the 2036 start date and outline possible gameplay paths for Iranian internal politics within the TLWW framework.

---

I. DESIGN PREMISE

Iran occupies a unique geopolitical position in the TLWW setting.

Unlike many states whose ideological direction may be relatively predictable by 2036, Iran sits at the intersection of several potential historical trajectories.

Real-world developments suggest multiple plausible futures for the Iranian state:

• Continued theocratic governance
• Military-dominated revolutionary state
• Moderated nationalist republic
• Hybrid authoritarian-democratic regime similar to modern Turkey

For TLWW design purposes, Iran should begin the game in a condition of **internal ideological tension**, rather than as a fully stabilized regime.

This reflects the long-term pressures acting on the Iranian political system.

---

II. STARTING SITUATION (2036 BASELINE)

Recommended baseline configuration:

Government Type
Multipolar-aligned nationalist government

Political Environment
Fragmented elite structure with multiple competing factions.

Key internal actors:

Revolutionary Guard Establishment
Security elite with influence over defense policy and foreign alignment.

Clerical Legacy Institutions
Religious authority structures inherited from the Islamic Republic.

Technocratic Nationalists
Administrative and economic reform advocates seeking greater global integration.

Iran in 2036 should therefore begin as a state with **internal factional competition rather than ideological uniformity**.

---

III. MAJOR POLITICAL FACTIONS

The following factions represent possible political outcomes.

1. Reformist National Republic Movement

Ideology
Atlanticism or pragmatic non-alignment

Characteristics

Secularized governance
Economic modernization
Partial political liberalization
Reduced clerical influence

Foreign Policy Orientation

Attempted normalization with Western states
Balancing between Atlantic and Multipolar powers

Gameplay Implications

Potential diplomatic opening toward Atlantic-aligned states
Domestic resistance from religious and nationalist factions

---

2. Revolutionary Guard Directorate

Ideology
Multipolarity

Characteristics

Security-dominated governance
State-controlled economic sectors
Strategic partnership with Russia and China

Foreign Policy Orientation

Alignment with Multipolar bloc
Expansion of regional influence networks

Gameplay Implications

Strong military state
Potential conflicts in Middle East power balance

---

3. Islamic Restoration Front

Ideology
National Integralism

Characteristics

Revival of theocratic authority
Strong religious nationalism
Civilizational framing of Iranian state identity

This path represents a restoration or radicalization of the original revolutionary ideology of the Islamic Republic.

Foreign Policy Orientation

Civilizational Islamic bloc formation

Possible partners

Pakistan
Afghanistan
Elements of North African political movements

Important Note

Despite sharing Integralist characteristics, this government would not naturally align with Western Integralist states due to civilizational differences.

---

IV. GAMEPLAY DESIGN GOALS

Iran should function as a geopolitical wildcard rather than a fixed member of any global bloc.

Desired gameplay outcomes:

Iran may align with Multipolar powers

Iran may partially normalize relations with Atlantic states

Iran may lead an Islamic civilizational bloc

Iran may become internally unstable or fragmented

This flexibility increases replayability and reflects Iran’s historically complex political evolution.

---

V. CIVILIZATIONAL CONTEXT

Under the TLWW Civilization System, Iran belongs to the Islamic civilizational sphere.

This means:

Integralist Iran will not automatically align with Integralist Atlantic states.

Instead it may pursue independent civilizational leadership within the Islamic world.

This reinforces TLWW’s core theme of civilizational competition.

---

VI. LONG TERM EVENT POTENTIAL

Possible future event chains:

Succession crisis within clerical leadership

Revolutionary Guard power struggle

Popular reform movement

Regional civilizational leadership campaigns

These dynamics could trigger major political shifts in Iran during gameplay.

---

END OF NOTE
